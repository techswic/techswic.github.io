Who are we?

Who are we?

Consultants. Guide. Mentors.

Founded in 2020, Techswic is a small step towards making research, mentoring, and consulting services in computer science more affordable and accessible to all. We provide these services at industry best charges while maintaining the highest standards of work. While many saw obstacles during the 2020 pandemic, we saw a window of opportunity: to use technology to reach out to more people, and to make quality services be known to the remotest of areas, otherwise deprived of them. We have started as a tech consultancy services company, and have huge plans towards slowly launching edtech products and self-paced courses in the future.

Why the name Techswic?

A Tech company, where swic is an acronym for our four services: skilling, writing, innovation, and counseling. Swic is also homophonic to Swack, a Scottish word for flexibility and grace. We make your tech more flexible and elegant :)

Team

Currently, we are a team of four with new members being added as we continue to grow. We are also open to collaborate with contractors who work on project basis with relevant prior experience.
